PERSONAL REPAIR SERVICES PROGRAM

We now offer our volunteer Personal Repair Services Program twice monthly, by appointment only, for facility-based seniors needing item repairs. This arose from consistent needs observed during service work in retirement residences, where our techs found themselves doing as much work for residents as for the administration during original service calls.

We've decided to separate this part of our business out of the day-to-day operations and try to give back in a small way to those who came before us.
