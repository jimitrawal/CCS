OUR HISTORY

Our History

Canadian Contractor Services (CCS) has been serving the Edmonton area for over 15 years. Founded in 2009 by John Smith, our company started as a small family-owned business specializing in residential renovations. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include commercial projects, insurance inspections, and office installations.

Our success is built on a foundation of trust, quality craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction. We have completed hundreds of projects, ranging from small home repairs to large-scale commercial developments. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results on every job.

As we look to the future, CCS remains committed to providing reliable and affordable contracting services to the Edmonton community. We continue to invest in our team, technology, and processes to ensure that we meet the evolving needs of our clients and exceed their expectations.
